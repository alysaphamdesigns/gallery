final project

This is my final project which remains untitled for now. For this final project, I wanted to combine mediums that I was used to and newer to. I really enjoyed learning coding in this class and knew that I wanted my piece to be interactive with p5.js. I love making pieces that have collage work or require putting pieces together - which I believe reflects how life and identity moves and comes in pieces. For this project, I dug through old photographs of my face. From baby Alysa to young adult Alysa. Through trial and error and figuring out what pieces made the most sense I ended up with a nice template in Adobe Photoshop and then translated my template into P5.js. Once translated in code, I still wanted my piece to have interactiveness. Through experimentation of clicking and dragging, hovering, rollovers and with the help of Githubs co- pilot, I was able to create an interactiveness that felt organic to life. The pieces from my life, I still carry with me and they hover around me. Ultimately, this is the foundation of who I am and at the end of the day (with one click) I can revert back to who I was but with growth (a move of the mouse) I can unmask who I’m growing into. Relating to the use of mediums, p5.js is new to me and required me to grow while Adobe Photoshop is a medium I’ve been more familiar with - which also relates to growth in identity as a person and an artist.
