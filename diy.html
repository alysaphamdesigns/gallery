coding

Using code, I've created a drawing board and a drawing example of what my drawing board can do. My tool bar(press keys 0-9)starts off with very simple drawing tools in black and gray and then the later tool in my tool bar are a little bit more unconventional. I wanted to make a tool bar that could produce high contrast drawings because I love the use of mixed media and unconventional tools. I remembered in one of my past art classes my teacher wanted us to draw with sticks and ink instead of brushes because he wanted us to use something unconventional to create a piece to show that you don't need expensive tools in order to create something beautiful. I believe my tool bar can produce the same effect/feeling! You can click the key 'X' to erase and the keys '0-9' to draw.
