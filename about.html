hey you!

My name is Alysa Pham. I am currently enrolled at SJSU for their Graphic Design major. I plan to pursue a BFA in graphic design here at San Jose. I am a Bay Area native and draw major inspiration from my home and its culture. Growing up in the Bay Area, allowed me to be exposed to such wondrous different cultures that all end up blending together to create a beautiful rich cohesive community. Much like the street art here in the Bay, the walls are covered in a mix of art styles from graffiti, to photorealism murals but somehow come together to create one cohesive piece. I strive to make designs that are experimental but can be used on everyday occasions that feature the mixing of media and styles. I have experience in Adobe programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign. I also work in traditional media such as graphite and charcoal. As I branch my skills out here at SJSU, I have been learning code through HTML and P5.js and I look forward to learning more.
