glitch art

This piece was made by Data Bending the original image through a text editor app. I chose to glitch an image of Kewpie dolls because I love the design of them in general. I think that Kewpies, depending on the eye of the beholder, can be seen as sinister or cute. For me, I think they are sweet and delicate looking, but on the opposing side, my partner sees them as creepy and unsettling. I thought the glitch would be an interesting appearance as they distort the cuteness into maybe something else.
