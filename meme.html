meme mashup

This meme mashup was made using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. I decided to combine two trending memes- Moodeng the hippo and the "I want to be part of your symphony" meme. I thought that this would be a good combination as both things are super cutsey and fun while conveying a sort of dark message. I also think the development of Moodeng (growing into a not so cute adult and going through puberty), is so real and adds another element to the meme.
